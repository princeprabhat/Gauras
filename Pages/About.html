ABOUT GAURAS

The Purest Form Of A2 Milk And Organic Products In Your Town

Our Story

Gauras, founded in 2020 with the mission of "From Farm to Your Home," has evolved into a trusted name that represents all things pure and natural. We specialize in selling organic Indian cow breed products and pride ourselves on our unique perspective towards what we do. Our products are more than just commodities; they provide nourishment and sustenance. That's why we ensure that every bit of our products is packed with all of their natural goodness, from the farm to your home, without any middleman involvement under our strict supervision, guaranteeing high quality. Our farm, located in a rural area, about 5 km from Neamatpur, provides a pure and unpolluted environment for our pure breed Gir cows, which are fed with natural grasses, leaves, and other hygienic materials like carrots, beetroots, cane, and more. We maintain a healthy and open environment for our cows and do not use growth hormone injections to increase milk production. Our cows undergo bi-monthly check-ups from a team of veterinary doctors, ensuring that you receive the utmost purity. At Gauras, we specialize in producing and promoting natural, wholesome, and healthy food and bi-products that are free from harmful toxins, fertilizers, and chemicals. We carefully source and make all of our products with hygiene and love.

OUR MISSION

Gauras aims to educate individuals about the incredible health benefits of products made from Indian cow breeds. Through nurturing and milking our desi cows, we provide access to fresh, unadulterated, pure, and organic A2 protein products. Our dual mission is to save our indigenous cow breeds and promote good health and well-being for all.

Total Transparent Access To Our Farm

Our philosophy is centered around transparency and doing what is right. To ensure our customers are fully informed, we offer complete access to our farm and welcome them to visit at their convenience.

Embracing a Holistic and Nourishing Lifestyle

Gauras products offer the purest and most natural form of dairy, free from any additives. Moreover, they are rich in A2 beta-casein protein, known for its potential health benefits.
